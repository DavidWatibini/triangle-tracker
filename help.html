Triangle Tracker Help

hey having trouble finding your way through the triangle Checker? follow the following help instructions so as to be effective.

  1. Input values into all the fields provided.
  2. Click on the submit button
  3. view answer on the right side of your page.
  4. Click on the reset button so as to enable a fresh input.
  5. Click on the clues button to run possible answers.
  6. Your answers should either be; an Equilateral triangle, Isoceles triangle, Scalene triangle or not a triangle.
  7. An Equilateral triangle has all three sides equal.
  8. An Isoceles Triangle has two sides equal.
  9. A Scalene Triangle has no sides equal.
